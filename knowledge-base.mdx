
# Creating Your Knowledge Base

## Overview
The knowledge base helps Juju understand your design preferences and requirements better. This personalized information guides the AI in generating more accurate designs.

## Setting Up

1. Navigate to Account Settings
2. Select Knowledge Base
3. Enter your design preferences
4. Save your settings

## Best Practices

1. **Be Specific**: Detail your brand colors, styles, and preferences
2. **Include Examples**: Reference specific design elements you like
3. **Update Regularly**: Keep your preferences current
4. **Add Context**: Include industry-specific requirements

## Example Setup
```
I'm a game designer focused on retro pixel art and neon cyberpunk aesthetics. 
When creating designs, please use bold, vibrant colors with a dark background. 
I prefer minimalist layouts with a focus on pixel-perfect details and retro gaming elements.
```
